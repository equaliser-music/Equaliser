# Future Feature Proposals

Consolidated from early contributor discussions and mockup-phase brainstorming.
Items already implemented have been removed — see CLAUDE.md TODO section for completed work.

---

## Client Features

### Community & Social
1. **Listening Parties** — Real-time synchronized listening sessions where fans can hear new releases together with chat/reactions (better than Bandcamp's implementation)
2. **User Member Pages** — Public profiles showing playlists, listening history, and social activity (basic user.html exists; needs listening history and richer activity display)
3. **Transparent Playlisting** — Show why tracks appear in playlists (no hidden algorithms), curator attribution

### Discovery & Curation
4. **"First Listen" Exclusives** — Highlight tracks that are exclusive or premiering on the platform
5. **Fair Discovery Algorithm** — Surface smaller indie artists proportionally, not just the most-streamed
6. **Genre/Mood Browsing** — Beyond search, let users explore by vibe, scene, or location

### Payments & Support
7. **Zap Integration** — Connect NOSTR feed zaps to actual Lightning payments
8. **Direct Artist Tipping** — One-click tip button during playback
9. **Transparent Royalty Display** — Show fans exactly how their payment is distributed

## Content Node (Artist Dashboard) Features

### Rich Artist Pages
10. **Press Pack Builder** — Upload/manage photos, videos, bios, logos that media can download
11. **Video Content** — Support for music videos, live sessions, behind-the-scenes
12. **Events/Shows** — List upcoming gigs, link to ticket sales
13. **News/Updates Feed** — Artist blog posts or announcements

### Analytics & Transparency
14. **Royalty Breakdown Analytics** — Clear view of earnings per stream/download vs. platform fees
15. **Listener Demographics** — Anonymous aggregate data on who's listening (geography, etc.)
16. **Playlist Tracking** — See which playlists feature your tracks and curator info

### Community Tools
17. **Listening Party Scheduler** — Create and promote synchronized listening events from the dashboard
18. **Exclusive Content Management** — Mark certain tracks as "exclusive first listen"

## Strategic Vision

Guiding principles from early contributor discussions — not specific features but shaping values.

- **Multi-homing Strategy** — Coexist with Spotify and other platforms; rely on occasional exclusives rather than demanding full exclusivity
- **Community-Based Governance** — Grow a network of musicians and fans who co-create the solution, rather than a top-down corporate product
- **Non-Profit Orientation** — Open questions about funding while staying aligned with values
- **Anti-Corporate DIY Ethos** — Align with indie community culture; reduce pressure on artists to create endless non-music content, making songs and albums central again
- **UX-Focused Process** — Use personas (Artist, Label Owner, Fan, Playlist Curator) to define needs and flows before building
