# Feature Suggestions for Equaliser Mockups

Based on the contributor email summary and analysis of the current mockups.

---

## Client Features

### Community & Social
1. **Listening Parties** - Real-time synchronized listening sessions where fans can hear new releases together with chat/reactions (mentioned as better than Bandcamp's)
2. **User Member Pages** - Public profiles showing playlists, listening history, and social activity
3. **Artist Chat/Forums** - Discussion boards on artist pages for fan-artist interaction
4. **Follow Feed** - A timeline of updates from followed artists (new releases, posts, listening parties)

### Discovery & Curation
5. **Transparent Playlisting** - Show why tracks appear in playlists (no hidden algorithms), curator attribution
6. **"First Listen" Exclusives** - Highlight tracks that are exclusive or premiering on the platform
7. **Fair Discovery Algorithm** - Surface smaller indie artists proportionally, not just the most-streamed
8. **Genre/Mood Browsing** - Beyond search, let users explore by vibe, scene, or location

### Payments & Support
9. **Working Zap Integration** - Connect the Nostr feed zaps to actual Lightning payments
10. **Direct Artist Tipping** - One-click tip button during playback
11. **Transparent Royalty Display** - Show fans exactly how their payment is distributed

---

## Content Node (Artist Dashboard) Features

### Rich Artist Pages
12. **Press Pack Builder** - Upload/manage photos, videos, bios, logos that media can download
13. **Video Content** - Support for music videos, live sessions, behind-the-scenes
14. **Events/Shows** - List upcoming gigs, link to ticket sales
15. **News/Updates Feed** - Artist blog posts or announcements

### Analytics & Transparency
16. **Royalty Breakdown** - Clear view of earnings per stream/download vs. platform fees
17. **Listener Demographics** - Anonymous aggregate data on who's listening (geography, etc.)
18. **Playlist Tracking** - See which playlists feature your tracks and curator info

### Community Tools
19. **Listening Party Scheduler** - Create and promote synchronized listening events
20. **Direct Messaging** - Respond to fan messages/comments
21. **Exclusive Content Management** - Mark certain tracks as "exclusive first listen"

---

## Technical/Platform Features
22. **Multi-Artist Content Nodes** - Let labels/collectives share hosting infrastructure
23. **PRS/MCPS Integration** - Royalty reporting to collection societies
24. **Anti-Gaming Protections** - Prevent fake streams/bad actors
